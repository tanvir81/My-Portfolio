## Animation Techniques Used

1. **GSAP ScrollTrigger** - Scroll-based animations
2. **Framer Motion variants** - Declarative animation states
3. **Stagger animations** - Sequential element animations
4. **3D transforms** - Rotation and perspective effects
5. **Clip-path animations** - Creative reveal effects
6. **Gradient animations** - Animated color overlays
7. **Elastic easing** - Bouncy, natural motion
8. **Hover interactions** - Responsive micro-interactions
9. **Continuous animations** - Infinite loop effects
10. **Parallax effects** - Depth and movement

## Performance Optimizations
- Animations trigger only once on scroll (once: true)
- GPU-accelerated transforms (scale, rotate, translate)
- Optimized animation timing and easing
- Lazy loading with scroll triggers
- Efficient re-renders with refs

All animations are modern, smooth, and enhance the user experience without being overwhelming.
